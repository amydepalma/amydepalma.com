Progress Learning and Carney teamed up to develop the company's new site as they transitioned from USATestprep to their new brand, Progress Learning, to provide the company with a new marketing site to showcase their innovative document management solution.

One of the more complex aspects of this site was to automatically generate elementary, middle, and high school URLs for each of the 50 state's supplementary pages, while also keeping the content management under control. This was accomplished by leveraging custom post types, option pages for the various default content, advanced custom fields, and Wordpress's templating and rewrite rules.

I was the sole developer on this project where I was tasked with planning, project management, development, and go-live handoff to the client.
