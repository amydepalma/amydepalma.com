The Westmoreland Museum of American Art tasked Carney with overhauling the museum's online extensive home, from it's information architecture, Wordpress setup, and branding. Advanced Custom Fields Pro aided the creation of custom Gutenberg blocks throughout the site while the core Wordpress blocks were also utilized to aid in a smoother transition as the site updates.

I played the lead developer role on this project where I developed the project plan, delegated tasks, and participated in development.
